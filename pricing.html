How much would it cost to build this site?

Content Pricing

Black Gekko does not have exact rates becuase not every website is the same. We base the price on what you want for your site, a site with a single page would be much less than a website with five pages and a built in user portal. But dont let this discourage you, the average website with a home, about, and contact page, with a few little goodies would cost around $300 initaly, with a lower price per month/year.

When do I get charged?

Payments

We understand a new organization can seem untrustworthy, with this, we will alow our customers to make payments per month/week. This will allow the customer to not have to commit the full price in fear of things not getting done. These monthly/weekly prices will be discused during the planing phase of the website, this is also the time quoting will occur.
